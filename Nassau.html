Nassau

One of the three major cities that Edward Kenway visits in the game, alongside Havana and Kingston. Nassau goes through an interesting transformation throughout the course of Black Flag. At the start, it's a pirate republic that acts as a friendly hub to Edward and his compatriots. But at a certain point, the British besiege the city, and Nassau becomes a much more threatening, hostile location.
